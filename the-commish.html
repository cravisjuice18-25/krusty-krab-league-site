Franchise Story

About The Commish

The Commish is the league’s commissioner-run franchise, built on long-term relevance, deep playoff history, constant group chat accountability, and the burden of being blamed for every rule interpretation, platform issue, and league inconvenience.

The franchise has been one of the league’s most consistent contenders, but that consistency has also come with painful championship losses, high expectations, and a growing archive of almost-glory.
